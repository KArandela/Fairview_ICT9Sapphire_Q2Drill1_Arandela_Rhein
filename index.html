What is a Go‑Bag?

It’s a pre‑packed bag with supplies for at least 72 hours. Keep it in an easy‑to‑grab place near your exit.

  • ✔ Keep copies of IDs and emergency contacts sealed in a Waterproof pouch.
  • ✔ Rotate food, water, batteries, and meds before they expire.
  • ✔ Customize for kids, pets, seniors, and local hazards.

Core supplies

Water

Hydration

Minimum 3L per person/day. Include purification options.

  • 3× 1L bottles or collapsible bladders
  • Water purification tablets/filter
Food

Calories

No‑cook, long‑life snacks and meals.

  • Energy bars, trail mix
  • Canned goods + opener
First Aid

Medical

Treat minor injuries and stabilize.

  • Bandages, gauze, tape
  • Antiseptic, pain reliever
  • Personal meds (7 days)
Tools

Multi‑use Gear

Compact tools for utility and safety.

  • Flashlight + extra batteries
  • Multi‑tool / duct tape
  • Whistle / lighter / matches
